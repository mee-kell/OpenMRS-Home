The global OpenMRS community works together to build the world's leading open source enterprise electronic medical record system platform.
We respond to those building and managing health systems in the developing world, where AIDS, tuberculosis, and malaria afflict millions of people.
Our mission is to improve healthcare delivery in resource-constrained environments, by coordinating a global community to create and support our software.

Who we are

We are a worldwide network of volunteers from various backgrounds, including technology, health care, and international development. Together, we're building the world's largest and most flexible technology platform to support healthcare delivery in challenging environments.


Where we've gone

Although we started out to fix a database system in a single clinic in Kenya, in the last few years OpenMRS has grown dramatically to be used in research and clinical settings around the planet. We're very proud of the innovators using OpenMRS to improve health care worldwide.
